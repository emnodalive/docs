To make sure your payment link integration works as expected, use test card data below. This way, you can simulate successful payments without having to use real money.

<Info>
  Do not use real card details to test your payment links.
</Info>

## How to use test cards

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    Log in to your [Rocka Hub](https://hub.rocka.live).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2">
    On the left sidebar, click **Payment Links**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3">
    Click **Generate a test card link**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4">
    Use the test credit card details from the table above to test your payment link in sandbox mode.
  </Step>
</Steps>
